Fengchao Yu

University of Michigan, Ann Arbor, Michigan, USA

I am a research fellow from Alexey Nesvizhskii's lab at University of Michigan. I got my PhD degree from Weichuan Yu's lab at The Hong Kong University of Science and Technology. My research interests include Proteomics, Bioinformatics, computational biology, machine learning, and statistics.
